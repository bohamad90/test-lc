Victron Instant Readout — Protocol Test Standalone test page. Open this in Bluefy on iPad/iPhone (Bluefy's watchAdvertisements support is required), or Chrome on desktop/Android. This does not connect to your Victron device — it passively listens to the encrypted status broadcasts it's already sending. You need the device's encryption key first: open VictronConnect → your device → gear icon → Product Info → Instant Readout via Bluetooth → enable it → tap Show under Instant Readout Details.
